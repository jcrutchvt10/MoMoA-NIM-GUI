MoMoA Control Room

Mixture of Mixture of Agents

Coordinate independent LLM experts to solve complex, long-running engineering tasks that exceed the capabilities of a single-agent loop.

MoMoA breaks large projects into sub-tasks assigned to dynamic Work Phase Rooms. Inside each room, two opposing experts — such as a Creative Developer and a Conservative Senior Engineer — debate and validate each other's work before reporting to an Orchestrator.

A background Overseer fires every 15 minutes to detect and break circular logic loops.

MoMoA runs a WebSocket server that handles all AI calls. Enter your MoMoA server endpoint (default ws://localhost:3007) and your NVIDIA NIM API key. These are saved only in this browser and sent directly to your server, never to GitHub.
